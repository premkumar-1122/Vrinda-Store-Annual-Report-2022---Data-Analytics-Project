# 📊 Vrinda Store Annual Report 2022 - Data Analytics Project

## 📝 Project Overview

This project showcases a complete end-to-end data analytics workflow using **Microsoft Excel**. The goal was to analyze the 2022 annual sales data for Vrinda Store, identify key trends, and create an interactive dashboard to help the business make data-driven decisions.

By utilizing dynamic slicers, the dashboard allows stakeholders to filter data by **Month**, **Channel**, and **Category**, instantly updating the visuals to reflect the selected criteria.

## 🛠️ Skills & Techniques Demonstrated

- **Data Cleaning:** Removing duplicates, handling missing values, and ensuring data consistency.
- **Data Processing:** Creating new columns, categorizing data (e.g., grouping ages into Adult, Senior, Teenager), and formatting.
- **Data Analytics:** Utilizing Pivot Tables and formulas to aggregate and summarize large datasets.
- **Data Visualization:** Designing intuitive charts (Combo charts, Pie charts, Bar charts, and Clustered Column charts) to represent complex data simply.
- **Interactive Dashboarding:** Connecting multiple Pivot Charts with Slicers to create a responsive, single-page executive summary.

---

## ❓ Business Questions Answered

The dashboard was built to answer the following critical business questions:

1. What was the overall trend of sales and orders throughout the year 2022?
2. Which gender contributes the most to the total sales?
3. What is the current status of all orders (Delivered, Refunded, Returned, Cancelled)?
4. Which geographical states generate the highest sales revenue?
5. How does the age and gender demographic correlate with order volume?
6. Which e-commerce channels (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, etc.) are the most profitable?

---

## 🔍 Key Insights & Findings

Based on the dashboard analysis, several actionable insights were discovered:

| Category | Insight |
|---|---|
| 📅 Peak Sales Period | Sales and order volumes peaked around **March**, followed by a gradual decline towards year-end. |
| 👩 Target Audience | Women account for **64%** of total sales. Adult Women (~34.59%) are the largest single buying demographic. |
| 🗺️ Top Geographies | **Maharashtra** leads with ₹2.99M, followed by **Karnataka** (₹2.65M) and **Uttar Pradesh** (₹2.10M). |
| 🛒 Channel Performance | **Amazon** (35%), **Myntra** (23%), and **Flipkart** (22%) collectively drive **80%** of total orders. |
| ✅ Operational Efficiency | **92%** of orders are successfully delivered, with low return and cancellation rates of just 3% each. |

---

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations

Based on the data insights, the following next steps are recommended for Vrinda Store:

1. **Targeted Marketing:** Direct campaigns and promotional offers heavily towards **Adult Women**, as they are the highest converting demographic.
2. **Inventory & Supply Chain:** Increase inventory distribution and local marketing in top-performing states — **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh** — to further capture market share.
3. **Channel Optimization:** Continue nurturing strong relationships with **Amazon, Myntra, and Flipkart**, while investigating why other channels (like Meesho and Nalli) are underperforming.
4. **Seasonal Promotions:** Since sales dip in later months, introduce special **Q4 promotions (October–December)** to boost end-of-year revenue.

## 💻 How to Use the Dashboard

1. Download the `.xlsx` file from this repository.
2. Open the file in **Microsoft Excel**.
3. Navigate to the **Dashboard** sheet.
4. Use the **Slicers** on the left-hand side (**Month**, **Channel**, **Category**) to filter the data.
5. The charts will update **dynamically** based on your selections!
